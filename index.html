Human History

The history of humanity is a remarkable feat,
starting from a species of hunter-gatherers to
engineers and the creation of a digital world.
Often times the timeline can be blurry and hard
to fully visualise. This site provides a full
comprehensive timeline of the history of humanity
from its emergence to modern day, with focus
globally and categorised into multiple important
eras/periods that shifted life. This is 300k
years of history summarised. To proceed, start
with preferably the Human Prehistory page shown
at the top of the screen for a timeline order,
or feel free to read about any specific time period
you wish.
